I'm a maker - a creative support pro and occasional web developer with an eye for design and a passion for solving problems. Overall, I'm an adaptable generalist and will learn and use the tools needed to solve the problem I'm working on. I'm all about about expanding skills and tackling new challenges!

I studied History in college and worked in the insurance industry for four years afterwards. Then I took a big leap and delved into web development full-time, something that had previously been a hobby for me... back to my first GeoCities site in junior high! (It was an extremely rad Harry Potter fansite.) But I missed having a direct connection with customers, so I'm now looking for a nice balance of support and tech.

In my free time, I keep making things - I love to knit, play video games, bake huge cakes, and read a large number of books..

I live in the beautiful Pacific Northwest (WA, to be specific) with my favorites: a person, a dog, and a cat.
